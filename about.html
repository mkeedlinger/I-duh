## Ido _todo_ ## FAQ #### How is 'Ido' pronounced? Ido is pronounced `/ˈiːdoʊ/` using the [IPA](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Help:IPA_for_English) (International Phonetic Alphabet) for English. For those of you who didn't previously know about the IPA or how to read it, `'` signifies that the primary stress follows it, `i:` is a long 'e' sound as in 'seed', `d` is a 'd' sound as in 'dye', and `oʊ` is a long 'o' sound as in 'bode'. The `/` simply signifies the beginning and end of the use of IPA. Simply, Ido is pronounced eedo. ## This site This site was created by [Michael Edlinger](https://plus.google.com/u/0/+MichaelEdlinger88) and [Jesse Smick](https://plus.google.com/u/0/+JesseSmick) out of a need for better Ido resources and a more central Ido community. We had seen that there were a few sites out there that had some Ido resources, but they were all incredibly old and seemingly abandoned. We also liked [duolingo](https://www.duolingo.com/) and we severly dissapointed that there was no course for Ido! We thought that there should certainly be a resource for Ido the was as awesome as duolingo. We hope that this site can become the hub of Ido, both for learners and for fluent speakers. ## Contributing _note: We're in the process of learning Ido, but are currently only fluent in English, so you'll have to communicate with us in English.
